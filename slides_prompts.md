## Slide 4 — Tips for a Great Practice Session

**Prompt:**
Create a slide titled "How to Get the Most Out of Alex".

Content — two-column layout:

Left column: "DO ✅"
- Give Alex a specific math problem and see how he solves it
- Ask him to explain his reasoning: "Why do you think that?"
- Correct him step by step, just like with a real student
- Try different problem types: fractions, percentages, word problems

Right column: "THINGS TO KNOW ℹ️"
- Alex is powered by a fine-tuned LLaMA model — he stays in character as a confused student
- The model runs on a Render backend; responses stream in real time

- Each new chat starts fresh with a new conversation context

Bottom banner quote (highlighted):
"The best way to learn to teach is to teach — even if your student is an AI."
